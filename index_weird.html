How To Play Voyage


          
Core Mechs

          
Fish to earn coins, buy upgrades, fight ships, and feed your crew while you push deeper.


          
Basic Controling

          
    
            
  • Move your boat with WASD or arrow keys
    • 
            
  • Press F to fish when sailing (you can't fish near shops!)
    • 
            
  • To save, go to a shop and press the save & return button
    • 
            
  • To feed your crew, click on the crew button in the bottom right
    • 
            
  • Near docks (brown squares) and shopkeepers, press F to dock, board, or trade
    • 
            
  • On mobile, use the on-screen controls
    • 
          


          
Crew Rules

          
    
            
  • Crew hunger drops over time
    • 
            
  • Feed your crew from fish inventory before hunger hits zero
    • 
            
  • If too many crew are brought to easy fights, carelessness can cause great losses
    • 
          


          
Progression Tips

          
    
            
  • Upgrade rod first for easier fishing
    • 
            
  • Upgrade sword before big fights to reduce losses
    • 
            
  • Use shops to convert fish into coins
    • 
            
  • Beware! If you get defeated in battle, your save gets wiped as you pass away.
    •
